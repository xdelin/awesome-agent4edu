## Quick Reference: The Movements

- **Movement I - Ignition (Beats 1-6, 10%):** Light the forge; destroy the ordinary world
- **Movement II - First Tempering (Beats 7-14, 20%):** Shape through failure
- **Movement III - Scattering (Beats 15-22, 25%):** Expand, harden, fragment
- **Movement IV - Brightest Burning (Beats 23-28, 25%):** Master, gather, choose
- **Movement V - Final Forging (Beats 29-34, 15%):** Converge, fail, transcend
- **Coda (Beats 35-36, 5%):** Reveal the tempered blade

## Strand Key

Use these abbreviations in the Strand(s) column:
- **Q** = Quest (external plot)
- **F** = Fire (internal transformation)
- **C** = Constellation (relationships)
- **FP0-FP3** = Forge Points (all strands converge)
- **Apex** = Willed Surrender (all strands fuse)
